# Common Interview Questions

## Architecture

1. What is layered architecture?
2. Why use a service layer?
3. What is the repository pattern?
4. Why are defensive copies useful?
5. What is the difference between a DTO and a domain object?

## Application workflows

1. How do you prevent half-updated state?
2. Where should authorization checks happen?
3. What should a failed operation do to existing state?
4. Why use custom exceptions?

## Build and CI

1. What does Maven do?
2. What is the standard Maven folder structure?
3. Why use Maven Wrapper?
4. What does CI verify?

## Persistence

1. What is ORM?
2. What is the difference between JPA and Hibernate?
3. What does `EntityManager` do?
4. Why are transactions needed?
5. How is JPQL different from SQL?

## Data import and reports

1. Why return an import result object?
2. Why avoid the default charset?
3. Why use logging instead of only `System.out`?
4. How should reports handle empty data?

## Quick summary

Good answers explain both what a concept is and why it improves maintainability, correctness, or testing.
